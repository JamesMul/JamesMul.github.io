About me

Hi, my name is James and I work as a Researcher/analyst. I hold a PhD in Cognitive and Affective Neuroscience, a Master's degree in Clinical Neurodevelopmental Sciences and a Bachelor's degree in Psychology.

I have a keen interest in conducting reserch and using data to draw meaningful insights that have real world positive impact; from idea conception, to capturing accurate data, through data preperation, analaysis and management, all the way to impactful dissemination.

In my free time, I like to keep active, beit cycling, swimming or golf. I have been a swimmer since a very young age and I currently coach a competitive squad of aspiring pro swimmers. I'm also swimming the English Chanel this year so have lots of cold water prep to do!

Expermiental paradigm

in psychopy

During my PhD I supervised a MSc student and designed a cardiac contigent fear conditioning paradigm. The code presents fearful stimuli at different phases of the cardiac cycle to investigate brain-body-emotion signalling. The code is written in Python, deployed in PsychoPy and integrates with SPIKE software (for ECG contingent stimuli presentation).
